Passive income


Passive income is money generated from investments, 
properties or side hustles.  The goal is to achieve 
a steady flow of cash without the daily commitment 
of a full-time job.
--www.nerdwallet.com/blog/investing/what-is-passive-income-and-how-do-i-earn-it/
